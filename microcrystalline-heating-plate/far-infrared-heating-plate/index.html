Far Infrared Heating Plate

Far Infrared Heating Plate utilizes far infrared radiation technology for heating, which can quickly and evenly transfer heat to the target object. Its high efficiency and energy-saving features make it widely used in industrial drying, medical treatment, commercial kitchens and building insulation. The heating plate reduces heat waste by emitting 8-14 micron far infrared radiation and has the advantage of instant heat.

Far infrared heating plate adopts far infrared radiation heating technology, which is able to directly heat objects or the environment by emitting far infrared radiation. Its working principle is based on the principle of far infrared radiation heating, the wavelength of the radiation is generally between 8-14 microns.

Main features

1. Convenient operation:the structure of far infrared heating equipment is simple, easy to install, operate and maintain, just according to the raw material selection of the appropriate radiation element, and the design of the appropriate drying channel can be .

2. Improve the quality of products:far infrared rays have a certain penetrating ability, so that the internal and surface molecules of the material at the same time to absorb the radiation energy, resulting in a self-heating effect, so that the water and other solvent molecules evaporate, and the heat is uniform, to avoid the uneven heating due to heat expansion of the resulting deformation or quality changes.

3. Energy saving:far-infrared heater energy-saving principle is the curing coating formed in the radiating surface of the heater electric paint coating because of its high surface blackness, can absorb a large number of radiant heat energy, and because of its high emissivity, can be absorbed into the radiant heat absorbed object easily absorbed far-infrared heat energy in the form of electromagnetic wave transmission.

4. Warming fast, no bright light, high rate of heat radiation:graphene far infrared microcrystalline glass heating plate warming fast, no bright light, high rate of heat radiation, fast transfer speed, good heat preservation effect, large heating surface, graphene far infrared heating, heat conversion rate of 99%, energy saving and power saving at about 40%.

5. Material characteristics:far-infrared microcrystalline glass heating plate using microcrystalline glass material, with good heat conduction properties and high radiation efficiency, can be converted into far-infrared radiation energy, rapid heating of the surrounding environment.

6. Long service life:the normal service life is more than 10,000 hours.

7. Wide application:far infrared heating technology has been widely used in paint, plastics, food, pharmaceuticals, wood, leather, textiles, tea, tobacco and other products or materials, such as heating and melting, drying, shaping, consumption, curing and other processes.


Application fields

1. Industrial production:used in the production process for heating, drying, baking and other processes to improve production efficiency and reduce energy consumption.

   2. Healthcare:Far infrared rays are considered beneficial to the human body and can be used for physical therapy and promoting blood circulation.

3. Catering:Used in cooking equipment, such as electric ovens, electric grill pans, electric grills, etc.

4. Sauna and spa centers:to provide a warm and relaxing environment, sometimes also used for physical therapy.

5. Sterilization equipment:Far infrared rays have a certain bactericidal effect, and can be used in sterilization cabinets and other equipment.

6. Greenhouses and incubation chambers:used to maintain a constant temperature, suitable for the growth of plants and microorganisms.

7. Laboratory and scientific research:used in experimental processes that require precise temperature control.

8. Tobacco processing:used in the drying and processing of tobacco.

9. Food processing:used in the baking and drying process of food.

10. Wood and furniture industry:used in the drying of wood and furniture molding.

11. Plastic processing:used in heating and molding of plastics.

12. Automotive industry:for heating and molding of automotive components.

13. Electronic equipment:for the heating and drying process of certain electronic components.


How it works

Nanoscale materials are constructed onto an electrically insulating substrate to form a film layer of 1-20 microns with a certain conductivity. When energized, the nano-micro molecules are allowed to rub together, generating heat energy, which radiates far-infrared rays through the microcrystalline glass plate as a carrier, so that the radiated object reaches a rapid warming of a kind of energy-saving electric heating plate.

Installation and Maintenance

1. Installation:According to the requirements of the equipment, the far-infrared heating plate can be installed in the appropriate position by means of fixed bracket, hanging bracket and so on.

2. Maintenance:Clean the surface of the heating plate regularly to avoid the accumulation of dust and dirt affecting the heating effect. Check the power connection and the working condition of the heating element to ensure the safe operation of the equipment.


Precautions for safety

1. Temperature control:In the process of use, attention should be paid to controlling the temperature of the heating plate to avoid overheating leading to equipment damage or safety hazards.

2. Protective measures:Make sure the electrical connection of the heating plate is good, avoid contacting water source to prevent the risk of electric shock.

What is Far Infrared Heating Plate?

A Far Infrared Heating Plate is a device that utilizes far infrared radiation technology for heating, by emitting far infrared radiation to directly heat an object or environment.


What is the working principle of Far Infrared Heating Plate?

Far Infrared Heating Plates emit far infrared radiation through a built-in heating element. The radiation wave penetrates the surface of the object and converts the energy into heat, thus heating the object.


What are the main application areas of Far Infrared Heating Plates?

The main applications include industrial drying, medical treatment, commercial kitchens and building insulation.


What are the main advantages of Far Infrared Heating Plates?

The advantages include high efficiency, uniform heating, energy saving, environmental protection and instant heating.


How fast do Far Infrared heating plates heat up?

Far Infrared heating plates can heat up quickly, realize instant heating and reduce the preheating time.


What is the energy-saving effect of Far Infrared Heating Plate?

Far infrared radiation can directly heat the object, reducing the air loss of heat, compared with the traditional heating method is more energy-saving.


How to install Far Infrared Heating Plate?

The Far Infrared Heating Plate can be installed in the appropriate location by means of a fixed bracket, hanging bracket, etc. The specific method will be determined according to the requirements of the equipment.


How to maintain the Far Infrared Heating Plate?

Maintenance includes regular cleaning of the surface of the heating plate, checking the power connection and the working condition of the heating element to ensure the safe operation of the equipment.


How is the heating effect of Far Infrared Heating Plate?

Far infrared heating plates can evenly heat the surface of an object and penetrate the object to heat the interior, which is suitable for a wide range of materials and thicknesses.


What safety matters should I pay attention to when using Far Infrared Heating Plates?

You need to pay attention to control the temperature of the heating plate to avoid overheating; ensure that the electrical connection is good, avoid contact with water to prevent the risk of electric shock.
