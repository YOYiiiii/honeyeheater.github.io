Heated Gloves

Heated Gloves use advanced electric heating technology to provide long-lasting warmth to the hands. Users can adjust the temperature of the gloves through an intelligent temperature control system based on the ambient temperature and personal needs, ensuring that their hands remain comfortable even in cold weather. Suitable for various outdoor activities such as skiing, hunting, skating, cycling, etc., it is an ideal choice for keeping warm in winter.

Product Features

1. Heating Function:The heated Gloves have a built-in heating element that generates heat through battery power to provide continuous warmth to the hands. Users can adjust the heating level through the controller on the gloves or smartphone apps, and generally have multi-step temperature control to meet the warmth needs in different environments.

2. Warmth:Heated gloves are usually made of high-quality fabrics, such as polyester fiber, nylon, etc., with good warmth and wind resistance. Some high-end products will also use waterproof and breathable materials to ensure that your hands stay dry and warm even in rain or snow.

3. Comfort:The inner padding of the gloves is soft and comfortable, such as fluffy or cotton material, to ensure that you will not feel uncomfortable when wearing them for a long time. The cut and design of the gloves is ergonomic, ensuring that the gloves fit snugly on the hand without compromising dexterity.

4. Safety:The heating element is made of safe materials and has undergone strict safety tests to ensure that it will not cause harm to the human body in the process of use. Gloves with overheating protection, when the temperature exceeds the set value will automatically cut off the power, to prevent overheating caused by security risks.

Usage Scene

1. Outdoor activities:such as skiing, hiking, riding, etc., the heated gloves can effectively resist the cold and keep your hands warm.

2. Workplace:workers in cold environments, such as construction workers, couriers, etc., can wear heated gloves to prevent hand frostbite.

3. Daily life:for people who are prone to cold hands, such as the elderly or the physically weak, heated gloves are also a good choice for keeping warm in winter.


Maintenance and care

1. Charging and power management:Use original or safety-compliant charging equipment and mobile power for charging. Disconnect the power supply connection in time after charging is completed to avoid long time charging affecting the battery life.

2. Cleaning and Maintenance:Remove the battery and heating element before washing to avoid damage. Choose the appropriate cleaning method according to the instructions on the washing label on the gloves, which can usually be hand-washed or machine-washed (using a laundry bag). Air dry naturally after washing, avoid using a dryer or iron.

3. Storage and Custody:When not in use, fold the heated gloves and store them in a dry and ventilated place, avoiding direct sunlight or humid environments. Regularly check the heating elements and cords for signs of damage or deterioration, and replace or repair them promptly if necessary.


What are Heated Gloves?

Heated Gloves are gloves with a built-in heating element that generates heat via battery power to provide hand warmth, especially for use during cold seasons or outdoor activities.


What are the main features of Heated Gloves?

The main features include heating function, warmth, comfort and safety. Heated Gloves can adjust the temperature as needed, are made of high-quality materials to ensure warmth and freedom of movement, and have a built-in overheating protection mechanism to ensure safe use.


What is the heating element of Heated Gloves usually made of?

The heating elements of Heated Gloves are usually made of conductive materials such as carbon fiber strands or metal wires, which generate heat quickly when electricity is applied.


How do I adjust the temperature of Heated Gloves?

Most Heated Gloves are equipped with a thermostat that allows the user to adjust the heat level using a button on the glove or a smartphone app. Some gloves offer multiple levels of temperature control to meet the warmth needs of different environments.


Are Heated Gloves suitable for use in rain or snow?

Yes, some of the high-end heated gloves are made of waterproof and breathable materials to ensure that your hands stay dry and warm even in rain or snow. However, please note that not all heated gloves are waterproof, so check the product description carefully when purchasing.


What is the battery life of the heated gloves?

Battery life varies by brand and model, generally ranging from a few hours to over ten hours. It depends on factors such as heating level, ambient temperature, and battery capacity.


How do I clean and care for my Heated Gloves?

Remove the battery and heating element before cleaning. Choose the appropriate cleaning method according to the instructions on the wash label on the gloves, usually hand or machine washable (using a laundry bag). Air dry naturally after washing, avoid using a dryer or iron. When not in use, store gloves in a dry, ventilated area.


Are Heated Gloves suitable for all people?

While heated gloves are beneficial for most people, for certain specific groups of people (e.g., those with serious illnesses or injuries to the hands), it is best to consult a doctor or professional before use.


How fast do heated gloves heat up?

The heating speed depends on the power of the heating element and the design of the glove. Generally speaking, high quality heated gloves are able to quickly reach the set temperature in a short period of time, providing instant warmth to the hands.
