About Us

A modern technology company that develops, designs, manufactures and sells heating products.

Technology empowers, warms the world, creates value

Dongguan honeye electrothermal technology Co., ltd. was established in 2001, is a set of development and design, production and manufacturing, sales of heating products of modern science and technology companies, the main products are epoxy resin heating plate, aluminum-based heating plate, mica heating plate, PTC heating plate, PI heating pad, silicone heating pads and so on. Belonging to the earliest batch of domestic companies in the heating industry, many products have now filled the gaps in the domestic alternative to imports.

The company has a number of professionals, after more than 20 years of technology accumulation precipitation, around the customer needs of continuous innovation, the implementation of strict quality control system, in the new energy industry, the semiconductor industry, the battery and security equipment industry, as well as aerospace and other industries to provide high-quality professional services. Our products and solutions have been applied in more than 100 countries and regions, creating a wide range of social, economic and environmental benefits.
