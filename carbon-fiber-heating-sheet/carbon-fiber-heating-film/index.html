Carbon Fiber Heating Film

Our Carbon Fiber Heating Film for Eye Massager combines advanced carbon fiber technology with flexible design to deliver a safe, efficient, and comfortable heating solution for all types of eye care and wearable health devices. With rapid heating, precise temperature control, and customizable options, it is the ideal choice for manufacturers seeking to enhance product performance and user satisfaction.

Inquire Now
  • Product Details

The Carbon Fiber Heating Film for Eye Massager is a cutting-edge, flexible heating element designed specifically for wearable eye care devices, such as thermal eye massagers, eye masks, and other health and wellness applications. Made from premium Carbon fiber material, this Heating Film offers superior durability, rapid heat-up times, and uniform temperature distribution — providing a comfortable and safe heating experience that helps relieve eye fatigue, improve blood circulation, and promote relaxation.

Key Features and Benefits

  • Flexible Carbon fiber heater:
    Crafted with high-quality carbon fibers, this film offers excellent flexibility and tensile strength, allowing it to conform easily to curved surfaces like eye massagers and masks without cracking or losing heating efficiency.

  • Fast and Even Heating Performance:
    The carbon fiber heating element heats up quickly within seconds, delivering uniform warmth across the entire surface. This consistent heat helps soothe tired eyes, reduce puffiness, and enhance comfort during use.

  • Durable and Corrosion-Resistant Material:
    The film is made with corrosion-resistant PVC layers and advanced lamination techniques, ensuring resistance against moisture, sweat, and wear over extended use in personal care products.

  • Safe and Stable Temperature Control:
    Designed to work safely at low voltages (customizable 5V–24V DC or AC), the film supports integration with external thermostats or temperature controllers for precise temperature management and overheat protection, ensuring user safety and preventing burns.

  • Lightweight and Ultra-Thin Design:
    At just a few millimeters thick, the Heating Film does not add bulk or weight to eye massager devices, maintaining user comfort and device aesthetics.

  • Customizable Size and Shape:
    We provide OEM/ODM services allowing customization of the heating film’s size, power, voltage, and shape to perfectly fit different eye massager models or other wearable health devices.

FAQ – Carbon Fiber Heating Film for Eye Massager

Q1: What is a carbon fiber heating film for eye massagers?

A: It is a thin, flexible heating element made from carbon fiber material designed to provide fast, even, and safe warmth specifically for wearable eye care devices such as thermal eye massagers and masks.


Q2: Is the carbon fiber heating film safe to use near the eyes?

A: Yes, our heating film operates at low voltage with built-in overheat protection and precise temperature control, ensuring safe and comfortable heat without risk of burns or discomfort.


Q3: Can the heating film be customized for different eye massager designs?

A: Absolutely. We offer OEM/ODM customization including size, shape, power, and voltage to fit various device specifications and design requirements.


Q4: What is the typical operating voltage and power of the heating film?

A: The film generally operates on low voltages between 5V to 24V DC or AC, with power ratings customizable from 5W up to 35W depending on the application.


Q5: How quickly does the heating film warm up?

A: It heats up rapidly, typically reaching the target temperature within 1 to 3 seconds, providing instant soothing warmth.


Q6: Is the Carbon fiber heating film durable and flexible?

A: Yes, it is designed to be highly flexible and durable, capable of bending repeatedly without damage, making it ideal for wearable eye care products.


Q7: Does the heating film emit infrared heat beneficial for eye care?

A: Yes, carbon fiber heaters naturally emit far-infrared radiation, which can improve blood circulation and relieve eye strain.
