Heated Jacket

Heated Jacket is a warm jacket with built-in heating elements that can adjust temperature in multiple levels, suitable for cold weather, providing long-lasting warmth. Whether it's outdoor exploration, daily commuting, or leisure activities, the Heated Jacket can be your ideal choice.

Product Features

1. Intelligent Heating Technology:Adopts advanced heating elements, usually integrated in the core body areas of the jacket, such as the front chest, back, and waist, to provide even heat distribution.Equipped with multi-step temperature adjustment function, users can flexibly adjust the heating level according to the ambient temperature and personal needs.

2. Convenient charging method:Charging via USB port, supports most mobile power sources (e.g. rechargeable treasure), making it easy to charge anytime and anywhere. When not heating, the USB port can also be used to charge mobile devices such as smartphones, realizing multi-purpose.

3. High-quality fabrics:The outer shell fabrics are mostly made of polyester, nylon or cotton blends, which are waterproof, windproof and wear-resistant.

The inner layer is made of materials with excellent warmth-retaining properties, such as ultra-fine shaker fleece, which effectively locks in heat and provides long-lasting warmth.

4. Humanized design:Hidden USB charging cable design, the charging cable is hidden in the pocket, which is beautiful and does not affect the wearing experience. Equipped with non-slip and wear-resistant soles (for Heated Jackets with shoes) to increase wearing safety. Adjustable cuffs and hems ensure that the cold wind is not easily penetrated, and at the same time to meet the needs of different body shapes.

5. Fashionable and versatile:The appearance design is simple and generous, with a variety of colors to meet the aesthetic needs of different users. Whether for outdoor adventures, daily commuting or casual gatherings, the Heated Jacket can be your fashionable choice.

How it works

The Heated Jacket has built-in miniature heating elements that generate heat through the action of an electric current and transfer it evenly to the jacket's thermal layer. The user can adjust the heating level and temperature via a remote control or the control buttons on the jacket for precise heat control. When the heating elements are in operation, they quickly transfer heat to the core areas of the wearer's body, such as the front, back and waist, helping to protect the user from the cold.


Importance

Maintaining a stable body temperature in cold weather is vital to the health and safety of the human body.The Heated Jacket provides a convenient and efficient warming solution for the wearer through its unique working principle. It not only quickly raises the body temperature, but also provides continuous warmth support over a long period of time, effectively preventing various health problems caused by the cold, such as cold and arthritis. In addition, the Heated Jacket is an indispensable piece of equipment for outdoor workers and athletes, helping them to maintain optimal performance in harsh environmental conditions, improving work efficiency and athletic performance.


Efficacy

  • Health Benefits

1. Preventing cold-related illnesses:In cold weather, maintaining a stable body temperature is essential to prevent cold-related illnesses such as colds and respiratory infections. Heated Jacket helps to protect the wearer from the cold by providing a stable heating effect and reduces health problems caused by hypothermia.

2. Promote Blood Circulation:The heating function of the Heated Jacket promotes blood circulation in the body, accelerating blood flow and helping to relieve muscle tension and stiffness caused by the cold. Wearing Heated Jacket for a long period of time also has a positive effect on improving the body's microcirculation and preventing cardiovascular and cerebrovascular diseases.

3. Relieve joint pain:For people suffering from arthritis and other joint diseases, cold weather often leads to aggravation of joint pain, and the heating function of Heated Jacket can act directly on the joints to provide warm care and effectively relieve joint pain and discomfort.

  • Comfort

1. Non-restrictive design:The Heated Jacket is designed to be comfortable to wear, with soft, breathable fabrics and a sensible cut that ensures a virtually non-restrictive fit. At the same time, the clever layout of the heating elements avoids affecting the overall comfort of the jacket.

2. Intelligent Temperature Control Adjustment:Users can adjust the heating level and temperature according to their own needs and the ambient temperature through the remote control or the control buttons on the jacket. This intelligent temperature control allows the wearer to enjoy the most comfortable temperature experience at all times.

3. Waterproof and windproof performance:Many Heated Jackets are also waterproof and windproof to keep the interior warm and dry in harsh weather conditions. This design not only enhances wearing comfort, but also increases the practicality and durability of the jacket.

  • Warming effect

1. Rapid heating:Once the heating function is turned on, the Heated Jacket provides almost immediate warmth to the wearer. This fast heating feature allows the wearer to enjoy the warmth without having to wait.

2. Long-lasting heat preservation:Through multi-step temperature adjustment and intelligent temperature control system, Heated Jacket can continue to provide the wearer with stable heating effect over a long period of time. This long-lasting insulation allows the wearer to stay warm and comfortable in cold weather.

3. Full Body Warmth:The heating elements of the Heated Jacket are usually located in the core areas of the body, such as the chest, back and waist. This design ensures that the wearer feels the warmth effect throughout the entire body, avoiding the problem of localized cold.


How does Heated Jacket work?

The Heated Jacket has a built-in heating element (e.g., carbon fiber strands or metal wires) that generates heat via battery power to provide warmth to the user. Users can adjust the heating level through the controller or smartphone app.


What seasons or occasions is the Heated Jacket suitable for?

The Heated Jacket is especially suited for cold seasons or occasions that require long periods of time outdoors, such as skiing, hiking, biking, fishing, etc. in winter. It provides constant warmth to the wearer in cold temperatures.


What is the battery life of Heated Jacket?

Battery life depends on the heating level, the ambient temperature and the design of the jacket. Generally speaking, at the lowest heating level, the battery will last from a few hours to a dozen hours. At higher heating levels, the battery life will decrease accordingly.


How do I clean the Heated Jacket without damaging the heating element?

Before washing, remove the battery and all wires. Then, choose the appropriate cleaning method according to the instructions on the jacket's wash label. Hand washing or machine washing (using a laundry bag) are both possible, but avoid using bleach or strong alkaline detergents. After washing, dry the jacket naturally and avoid using a dryer or iron.


Does the heating element of Heated Jacket cause discomfort or burns?

Heated Jackets from legitimate brands are designed with safety in mind, and the temperature of the heating element will be controlled within a safe range to avoid burns. At the same time, the jacket usually has multiple layers of fabric to separate the heating element from the skin to increase wearing comfort.


Is the Heated Jacket waterproof?

This depends on the design of the specific product. Some high-end Heated Jackets will be waterproof for rain and snow. However, not all Heated Jackets are waterproof, so please check the product description carefully before purchasing.


How to adjust the heating level of Heated Jacket?

Most Heated Jackets come with a controller or can be adjusted via a smartphone app. Users can choose the right level of heating according to the ambient temperature and their personal feeling.


Is the Heated Jacket suitable for everyone?

While Heated Jacket provides a solution for most people to stay warm in winter, for certain groups of people (e.g. those with sensitive skin, pregnant women or those sensitive to electromagnetic fields), it is best to consult a doctor or professional before use.


Is the heating element of Heated Jacket replaceable or repairable?

This depends on the design and warranty policy of the specific product. Some brands may offer heating element replacement or repair services, but the cost may be higher. Therefore, it is important to understand the warranty policy of the product before purchasing.


What are the additional features of Heated Jacket besides warmth?

In addition to basic warmth, some premium Heated Jackets may have other additional features such as windproof, breathable, pocket heating, cell phone charging ports, and so on. These features are designed to enhance the comfort and convenience of the wearer.
